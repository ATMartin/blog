Foreword

15 September 2014

Hi. I'm Alex (sometimes Lex) and I want to build software.

I grew up in a tech-centric family. My parents were both educators but my dad moved into tech administration early on in his career and my mom became the de-facto "computer person" at every school she taught at. Their combined experience and knowledge rubbed off on me, and from my first green-screen Apple to my slew of gadgets today I've been breaking things to get a better idea of how they work.

I've set for myself the goal of being proficient at developing simple software from scratch. I say "simple" because I don't believe complexity defines quality. I say "scratch" because I don't believe rails generate scaffold Blog makes you an engineer.

I've lurked in the development ecosystem most of my life but I know it's important for me to become more active. I want to contirubte but I need both the chops and the confidence to do so. I'm starting this blog to, in part, make myself more accountable.

I hope to share some of my projects, both completed and in-progress, here, as well as any nifty discoveries I make while learning.

You can follow me at @ADotMartin or fork me on GitHub at ATMartin.

Alex Martin
